WHO AM I?

I am a curious creative that loves refining my skills
to solve messy human problems and make cool things.

#1

I started out in healthcare and software support, back when the one thing I was sure of was that I had a passion for helping people.

#2

I was able to excel in my roles, but always ran out of challenges to take on. Without something to sate my curiosity and continually challenge me, I was never satisfied and looked for a better outlet.

#3

However, once I realized that I could apply all of my curiousity and focus to learning a skill with limitless challenges and possibilities, I was hooked. I always wanted a skill I could spend my life mastering, and I found that outlet in programming.

#4

To make the switch, I completed a four-month full-stack development program in addition to my self-study because I knew it would help me become the strongest possible developer.
